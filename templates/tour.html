Discover Algeria

Experience the best of Algeria in this comprehensive 7-day tour. From the bustling streets of Algiers to the serene Sahara Desert, discover the rich history, diverse landscapes, and vibrant culture of this North African gem.

Duration: 7 days, 6 nights

Daily Itinerary Overview

Day 1

Arrival in Algiers

Begin your Algerian adventure in the capital city of Algiers, known as 'Algiers the White' for its stunning whitewashed buildings.

Day 2

Exploring Algiers

Discover the rich history and culture of Algeria's capital city through its monuments, museums, and historic districts.

Day 3

Constantine - City of Bridges

Travel to Constantine, known as the 'City of Bridges' for its spectacular natural bridges spanning deep gorges.

Day 4

Timgad & Batna

Discover the well-preserved Roman ruins of Timgad and explore the city of Batna, gateway to the Aurès Mountains.

Day 5

Biskra & El Oued

Journey to the edge of the Sahara Desert, visiting the oasis city of Biskra and the unique 'City of a Thousand Domes', El Oued.

Day 6

Ghardaïa & M'zab Valley

Discover the unique architecture and culture of the M'zab Valley, a UNESCO World Heritage site with its pentapolis of fortified cities.

Day 7

Return to Algiers

Return to the capital for your final day in Algeria, with time for last-minute shopping and a farewell dinner.

Practical Information

What to Pack

  • Lightweight clothing for hot days
  • Warmer layers for cool evenings
  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Sun protection (hat, sunglasses, sunscreen)
  • Modest clothing for visiting religious sites
  • Travel adapter for electrical outlets

Travel Tips

  • The local currency is the Algerian Dinar (DZD)
  • Arabic and French are the main languages spoken
  • Respect local customs and dress modestly
  • Tipping is appreciated but not mandatory
  • Carry a copy of your passport and visa at all times
  • Check travel advisories before your trip
